7Wiseways is an online tutoring platform with qualified teachers from top IB Schools across the globe. We provide live One-on-One classes, which take place in our online classroom using online video chat and a shared whiteboard. The teacher and the student can interact with each other through the video chat and write on the shared whiteboard.

7Wiseways gives you access to the best teachers in the world, without any constraints on geography. Once you request a lesson, we will match you to a highly qualified personal tutor, who will take the first lesson. Once you are satisfied with the first lesson, your dedicated academic counselor will chart a detailed academic plan, tailor-made for you.
